A public specialized STEM school under the Egyptian Ministry of Education, providing advanced education for academically outstanding students.

About STEM School – Behera

STEM School – Behera is part of Egypt's national STEM system, designed to cultivate the next generation of innovators and researchers. Our residential environment fosters academic excellence, collaborative learning, and scientific inquiry.

  • Part of Egypt's national STEM initiative
  • Residential environment supporting academic focus
  • Emphasis on research and innovation
  • Integrated curriculum across all disciplines

Residential Life

Our residential system creates a supportive and structured environment where students can focus on academics while developing social responsibility and community engagement.

  • Comprehensive student support services

  • Structured academic living environment
